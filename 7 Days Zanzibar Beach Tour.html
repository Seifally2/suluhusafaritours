Frequently Asked Questions

The best time to visit Zanzibar is during the dry season, from June to October, when the weather is sunny and warm, perfect for beach activities and exploring the island. Another good period is between December and February, which also offers pleasant weather for a tropical escape.

Zanzibar is famous for its beautiful beaches, historic Stone Town, and spice farms. Other must-visit attractions include Prison Island, Jozani Forest (home to the rare red colobus monkeys), and the Rock Restaurant, located on a small rocky island off the coast.

Zanzibar offers a variety of activities, from relaxing on the beach to water sports such as snorkeling, scuba diving, and kitesurfing. You can also explore the rich culture and history of Stone Town, visit the famous spice farms, or take a boat trip to nearby islands like Mnemba or Prison Island.

Tour Plan

01

Day 1: Arrival in Zanzibar airport

On arrival in Zanzibar you will be met by our representative and transferred to Mtoni Marine Hotel . Overnight at Mtoni Marine hotel. (Dinner is at client’s own discretion)

02

Day 2: Beach time and free space

After breakfast you will commence the Stone Town tour. The 2-hour tour will take you to the most scenic parts of the Old Town. This is the Cultural heart of Zanzibar. Our guides are experienced and very well conversant with the rich history of the Island. The Old Town oozes magic and mystery and shafts and sharp light joust with deep shadows creating a fascinating world in the narrow streets.

Overnight and Breakfast, at Mtoni Marine hotel

UNESCO has recently declared Stone Town as one of the world’s historic cities, and it’s now a protected area in Zanzibar. The tour takes you to the former site of the slave market; the cathedral (Cathedral Church of Christ) built in its site in 1874, the Sultan’s palace and more. Relax at leisure at the beach side.

03

Day 3: Zanzibar Spice Tour

After breakfast pick up from hotel and transfer to Kizimbani Village for the Spice Tour.

For much of the year the scent of cloves is carried in the breezes from the clove buds drying the sun on road reside mats. The Spice Island as it is affectionately called has over forty of the most valuable tropical aromatic plants that were introduced over the last two centuries by the Oman Arabs.

The spices are grown in the spice grades in the hills north of Stone Town. Our knowledgeable guides will take you through this fascinating tour. Spices can be tasted and even purchased. Later driven to the North East Coast for Check in at Zamani Kempinski.

04

Day 4: Day on the beach

Spend the day enjoying beaches of East Coast and the Swahili way of living or at the resort’s swimming pool. Breakfast, Dinner and overnight at Melia Zanzibar hotel.

05

Day 5: Walking in Jozani Forest, Zanzibar

After Breakfast you will be picked up for visit to Jozani Forest – Half day. This forest is one of the last remaining sanctuaries in the world of the red colubus monkey. Wonderful photo opportunities and sightings of these rare creatures living in the forest reserves are possible on the pathways. After lunch, transfer to Melia Zanzibar for overnight.

06

Day 6: Relax at the Melia Zanzibar

Breakfast at the hotel Full day at leisure Overnight and Dinner at the hotel.

07

Day 7: Departure from Zanzibar

after breakfast checks out and transfer to Zanzibar Airport for your flight out of Zanzibar. end of 7 days Zanzibar Beach Holiday
